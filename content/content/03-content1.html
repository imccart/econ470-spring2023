In Part 1 of this module, we’ll revisit the MA data from Module 0. We’ll also introduce the MA quality rating program and how it plays out in real life.

Objectives

  • Understand the MA data and datasets required for this Module
  • Explain the MA star rating program and how the star ratings are calculated
  • Explain the MA quality improvement program

Activities

  • Clone the Medicare Advantage repository (if you haven’t already) and begin working with data.
