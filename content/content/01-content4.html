Part 4 continues our work with matching, reweighting, and regression, but in the context of real life data on hospital prices.

Objectives

  • Understand HCRIS data and explain hospital pricing
  • Calculate an ATE or ATT using regression, matching, and weighting using real-world pricing data

Activities

  • Continue working with the Hospital Cost Report Information System (HCRIS) repository.
  • Estimate the effect of hospital penalties on hospital pricing
